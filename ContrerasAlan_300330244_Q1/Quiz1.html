Popular Sports

Ice hockey

  • A game of pick-up hockey in progress at Esplande Park in Quebec City.
  • Ice hockey was named Canada's official winter sport in 1994.
  • The modern form of ice hockey began in Canada in the late 19th century.
  • It is widely considered Canada's national pastime.

Baseball

  • The world's first documented baseball game took place in Beachville, Ontario on June 4, 1838.
  • Although more strongly associated with the United States, baseball has existed in Canada from the very beginning.
  • Baseball is one of the most popular sports in the country.
  • The oldest baseball park in the world is still in operation in Labatt Park in Ontario.

Image of Baseball

Basketball

  • Basketball has very strong roots in Canada.
  • Basketball is a popular sport in parts of Canada, especially in Nova Scotia, Southern Alberta.
  • The modern basketball game was founded by a Canadian, James Naismith, in 1891 while working as a physical education instructor in the US.
  • Several players who took part in the first game came from Canada.
